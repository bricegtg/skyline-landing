Who We Are

INTRODUCING SKYLINE DRONES

Skyline Drones is a drone-powered exterior building cleaning company based in Manila, Philippines. We deploy autonomous cleaning drones to wash, maintain, and restore building facades — safely, efficiently, and without putting a single worker at height.

Our fleet of commercial-grade cleaning drones services high-rises, hotels, government buildings, industrial facilities, and public infrastructure — delivering a faster, safer, and more cost-effective alternative to traditional rope-access and scaffolding methods.

Whether it's a 5-storey office or a 55-storey tower, Skyline Drones provides full-facade coverage at a fraction of the time and cost — with zero human risk.

The Problem

WHY BUILDING OWNERS ARE LOOKING FOR A BETTER WAY

High Liability

Legal & Insurance Exposure

Rope-access and scaffolding crews work at extreme heights daily — exposing building owners and facility managers to significant liability, insurance premiums, and regulatory risk with every engagement.

Excessive Cost

Inflated Maintenance Budgets

Traditional facade cleaning requires scaffolding, cranes, boom lifts, or rope-access teams — all of which demand extensive setup, specialised labour, and premium insurance. The result: inflated costs that strain maintenance budgets.

Operational Disruption

Weeks of Scaffolding

Scaffolding installations block entrances, obstruct signage, disrupt tenants, and take weeks to erect and dismantle — causing prolonged disruption to building operations and tenant experience.
